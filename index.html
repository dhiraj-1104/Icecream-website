Assorted Ingredients

Vanilla

Vanilla ice cream is a classic frozen dessert made with cream, sugar, and vanilla flavoring, often derived from vanilla beans or extract. It has a smooth, creamy texture and a rich yet mild flavor, making it a popular base for various toppings and desserts.

Mongo

Mango ice cream is a tropical-flavored frozen dessert made with cream, sugar, and ripe mango puree or flavoring. It has a smooth, creamy texture with a sweet and tangy taste, capturing the essence of fresh mangoes.

Taro

Taro ice cream is a creamy, purple-hued frozen dessert made with taro root, cream, and sugar. It has a smooth texture and a subtly sweet, nutty, and vanilla-like flavor, making it a unique and popular choice in many Asian desserts.

Coconut

Coconut ice cream is a rich and creamy frozen dessert made with coconut milk, cream, and sugar. It has a smooth texture and a naturally sweet, tropical flavor with a hint of nuttiness, making it a refreshing treat.

Cocoa

Cocoa ice cream is a rich and creamy frozen dessert made with cocoa powder, cream, and sugar. It has a smooth texture and a deep, chocolatey flavor, offering a perfect balance of sweetness and indulgence.

Strawberry

Strawberry ice cream is a sweet and creamy frozen dessert made with real strawberries, cream, and sugar. It has a smooth texture with a refreshing, fruity flavor and a natural pink hue, making it a classic favorite.

Green tea

Green tea ice cream is a creamy frozen dessert infused with matcha, a finely ground green tea powder. It has a smooth texture and a slightly earthy, bittersweet flavor, offering a refreshing and unique taste.

Orange

Orange ice cream is a creamy frozen dessert made with fresh orange juice or zest, cream, and sugar. It has a smooth texture with a bright, citrusy flavor that is both refreshing and sweet.
